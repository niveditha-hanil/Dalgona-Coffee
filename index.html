Ingredients

  • Instant Coffee powder or Expresso Granules
  • Hot water
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Dalgona Making Process

Step 1: Add Coffee powder to Hot water
Add 2 tablespoons (tbsp) of coffee powder and 3 tbsp of sugar to 1 cup of hot water must be boiling hot.
Step: Whisk, Whisk , Whisk untiul the coffee mixture turmns fluffy.
Using a hand held mixer, whisk the coffee mixture turns creamy . It would be light brown in color. Dont be lazy to whisk.
Step 3: Serve it with hot/Cold milk
Serve the cofee cream with hot/cold milk. A straw and a spoon would be mandate. Now stir, stir, stir to enjoy this lockdown recipe!!!
